Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

Last updated: 11 January 2026

Aderrig Neighbourhood Watch (ANW) is a community-based, voluntary initiative created, managed and maintained by residents and volunteers of the Aderrig area. This website exists solely to support community communication, awareness and cooperation among residents.

All information published on this website is voluntarily provided by residents. ANW acts strictly as a community platform for recording and sharing information and does not verify, validate, investigate or independently confirm the accuracy, completeness or reliability of any content submitted by users.

ANW is not a security service, law enforcement body, emergency response service or monitoring organisation. This platform does not replace or represent An Garda Síochána, emergency services or any public authority, and it has no role in requesting, directing or enforcing action by such bodies.

By using this website, users acknowledge that any actions or decisions taken based on information obtained through the platform are made at their own discretion and risk. ANW, its administrators and volunteers accept no liability for any loss, damage or claim arising directly or indirectly from the use of information shared on this site.

Personal Data

Personal data provided by residents — including names, email addresses, eircodes, approximate addresses, phone numbers and other information voluntarily submitted — is used solely for legitimate community, organisational and communication purposes within the scope of ANW.

ANW does not sell, rent or share personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Data is accessed only by authorised administrators and is processed in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU Regulation 2016/679 – GDPR) and applicable Irish data protection law.

Community Sponsors

Aderrig Neighbourhood Watch may receive voluntary financial support from local businesses and organisations referred to as Community Sponsors. This support exists solely to help cover the costs associated with website hosting, technical maintenance and the continued operation of this community platform.

Community Sponsors have no access to resident information, personal data, reports, alerts, administrative systems or any operational functions of the platform. Sponsorship does not imply endorsement, recommendation or preferential treatment of any business or service.

Sponsor logos may be displayed on the website in a small, uniform and non-commercial format to acknowledge their support for the community.

Logo specification for Community Supporters

  • Format: PNG or WEBP
  • Recommended size: 400 × 200 pixels
  • Background: Transparent or white
  • Orientation: Horizontal logos preferred
  • File size: Under 200 KB

Logos are displayed in a small, uniform format to ensure a non-commercial, community-focused appearance.

Security and Audit Logging

For security, operational integrity and audit purposes, ANW records limited technical information relating to successful account logins. This may include the date and time of login, the associated residence (eircode), and a basic account identifier.

These records are used exclusively to:

  • Protect the platform against unauthorised access or misuse
  • Investigate security incidents or account abuse
  • Maintain the integrity of the community platform

Audit records are retained for a maximum period of six (6) months and are then automatically deleted. Access to these records is strictly limited to authorised administrators.

Community Alerts and Communications

Residents may opt in to receive community alerts and notifications. These communications may be delivered by email and, where implemented, by SMS, WhatsApp or similar channels.

Participation in alert systems is entirely optional and based on explicit consent. Residents may withdraw consent at any time through their account settings or by contacting an administrator.

User Rights

Residents have the right to request access to their personal data, request correction or deletion of inaccurate data, and withdraw consent where applicable, subject to legal and operational limitations.

Requests relating to personal data or privacy may be directed to an administrator via the contact details provided on this site.

By creating an account or continuing to use this website, users confirm that they have read, understood and accepted this Privacy Policy and Terms of Use. Users who do not agree should refrain from using the platform.

ANW reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy and Terms of Use from time to time to reflect legal requirements, operational changes or community needs. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically.
