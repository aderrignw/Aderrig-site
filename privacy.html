Privacy Policy

Aderrig Neighbourhood Watch (“ANW”) is a community-led initiative designed to promote safety, awareness and cooperation among residents. This website operates as an informational and coordination platform only.

Information we collect

  • Name and email address provided during registration
  • Eircode (used solely to determine local neighbourhood grouping)
  • Volunteering interests and community roles (optional)
  • Incident reports voluntarily submitted by residents

How information is stored

Information entered on this website is stored in Netlify (using Netlify Functions + Netlify Blobs storage). Data is not stored in your browser for persistence (no localStorage/database on the device), and is only accessed by this site for the features you use.

Because data is stored in Netlify, clearing browser data will not remove stored site information. Access to your data depends on site access and any admin rules set within the site.

Use of information

Information is used solely to support neighbourhood coordination, volunteering, elections and incident reporting within the Aderrig community. No data is sold, monetised or used for commercial purposes.

Incident reports

Incident reports are created and stored by residents for their own reference. Residents may choose to prepare and send reports by email to relevant authorities using their own email client.

Data protection

This site follows the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in terms of transparency, purpose limitation and data minimisation. As a community pilot project, this platform does not currently operate a central database.

Terms of Use

Community purpose

This website is provided for community coordination and awareness purposes only. It is not an emergency service and does not replace Garda or emergency services.

User responsibilities

  • Provide accurate and truthful information
  • Use the platform respectfully and lawfully
  • Do not submit false, misleading or malicious reports
  • Respect the privacy of other residents

Approvals and roles

Certain features of the site require administrative approval. Aderrig Neighbourhood Watch reserves the right to approve, suspend or remove registrations or roles where necessary to protect the community.
